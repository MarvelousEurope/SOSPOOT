Among the inhabitants of Olive Town, there are some that you can grow more intimate with. Once you grow closer to each other, you can start a relationship and even get married if you choose! Find that special someone to love and support, and who will be there to do the same for you.

  • Bridget

    Though she worries for her brother Damon’s future, Bridget always keeps a smile on her face and an animal friend close by. She works together with her mother at the Hoof House Animal Shop.

  • Damon

    A coffee aficionado who tends to take his brew black. Behind the motorcycle-riding, tough-guy façade, however, Damon’s love for stargazing never fails to shine through.

  • Reina

    The only thing that matches Reina’s passion for partying is her unrelenting enthusiasm for her work at the museum. Her knowledge of the exhibits is second to none, much to museum curator Gloria’s delight.

  • Emilio

    A laid-back fisherman who takes things in stride and doesn’t let life get him down. The ocean’s been his backyard for as long as he can remember, and he plans on keeping it that way.

  • Linh

    Together with her grandfather, Nguyen, Linh helps run Olive Town’s flower shop. While she may seem bashful at first glance, she’s a steadfast friend who cherishes those she cares about.

  • Iori

    A kindly, reliable young lord who has traveled to Olive Town from faraway lands. His admiration for charming landscapes compelled him to remain in this nature-rich locale. He spends his days with a fishing rod in hand, hoping to hone his angling skills.

  • Laura

    Lighthearted and easygoing, this tour guide’s knowledge of Olive Town has earned her the respect of residents and tourists alike. For Laura, family comes first—when she’s not working, she can often be found at the grocery store, helping out her frail father.

  • Ralph

    A man of few words, Ralph is Olive Town’s level-headed ranger, working to protect the town’s flora and fauna. In times of trouble, he remains calm and collected, ready to take the lead. Perhaps that's thanks to his history as an athlete...

  • Blaire

    The Capeside Bistro’s cheerful waitress. Though she’s since moved away from the city and back home to Olive Town, she still longs to return to the hustle and bustle to become the star she’s always wanted to be.

  • Jack

    This lackadaisical lad helps out at his family’s general store... but he’s usually too busy thinking about ways to help his hometown grow. His little sister, Cindy, doesn’t know what to do with him, but he's too good-natured to stay mad at.
